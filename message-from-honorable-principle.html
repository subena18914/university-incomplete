Message-From-Honorable-Principle

Professor Dr.Mohammed Mizanur Rahman
National Institute of Textile Engineering & Research
Chairman, Governing Body
(NITER)

My heartiest welcome to all the young and budding Engineers who have joined at National Institute of Textile Engineering & Research (NITER) which is the first Public Private Partnership (PPP) Education Institute in Bangladesh situatedat 30 km away from the heart of Dhaka city.NITER community is warm and friendly and offers the highest standards of pastoral care. Having developed its reputation for high quality holistic education in textile engineering, with basic philosophy of “Equip yourself”. NITERhas been a pool of meritorious students consistently for past six years. And hence it has always tried to facilitate quality textile engineering education to equip and enrich young men and women to meet global challenges in development, innovation and application of textile in the service of humanity.Its rich academic tradition has always attracted the most talented students who later contribute to the development and progress of the society. For ensuring quality education in textile, NITER owns the largest textile laboratories in Bangladesh and also has the easy access to the latest technologies in practice in different textile industries since it is directly run by Bangladesh Textile Mills Association (BTMA). An added advantage is that it is situated in the Textiles and RMG hub of Bangladesh which facilitates our students to keep close contact with different industries.

The composite unit of Student, Parents, and Society is our Customer, NITER will strive hard to provide pleasant ambience for moulding the young minds to scale greater heights in their chosen field study of the different branches of textile engineering . In our institute we give top priority to academic excellence, innovation, and discipline. An effective Training and newly established Placement Cell is in place to provide job placement to all our student for the various industrial sector under BTMA

A state-of-the-art and up-to-date infrastructure facilities and good learning atmosphere are the hallmarks of this Institute. I hope all the students admitted in this Institute will enjoy their study in the campus. With the help of highly qualified and dedicated staff members, we will be moulding the students to the required shape which will make them industry-ready and employable. Let us all come together, build together, and achieve together for tomorrow's Bangladesh.

Professor Dr. Mohammed Mizanur Rahman
President, BTMA
Principal, NITER
